A Seamless Design-to-Deployment Journey

Step 1: Lux & Layout Consultation Analyzing your architectural blueprints, structural data, and preferred tech parameters.
Step 2: Load Planning & Node Layout Mapping electrical loads, smart switch relays, and computing driver placement coordinates.
Step 3: 3D Illumination Simulation Photorealistic 3D light spread rendering to visualize the exact mood, brightness, and glare control before wiring.
Step 4: Hardware & Finish Selection Curating certified touch switch panels, high-CRI LED components, and smart sensors.
Step 5: Wiring Optimization Technical on-site supervision to ensure stable neutral wiring channels and safe isolated circuits.
Step 6: Precision Fixture Fitting Flawless cutting of linear profiles and absolute mechanical installation of magnetic tracks.
Step 7: Programming & Handover Setting up customized lighting scenes, voice integration routines (Alexa/Google), and final technical deep-check.
