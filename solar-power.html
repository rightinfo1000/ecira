Our Core Solar Energy Integration

A. On-Grid Solar Systems (With Net-Metering)

The premium engineering solution for modern residential structures to drastically reduce monthly electrical overheads. Your clean energy generation is synchronized directly with the government grid network.

How it helps: Excess power generated during peak day hours is sent back to the grid via an approved net-meter, giving you electricity credits that virtually bring your power bill down to absolute zero.

B. Off-Grid & Hybrid Solar Systems

Ideal for infrastructure frameworks facing inconsistent power stability or for clients demanding absolute energy independence. Hybrid setups combine grid connectivity with advanced battery backup storage banks.

How it helps: Safely stores excess solar units inside high-performance battery arrays, ensuring your integrated smart nodes, architectural lighting, and heavy appliances run seamlessly during night hours or grid outages.

C. Premium Structure Fabrication & Technical Wiring

A solar panel array is only as resilient as its physical foundation. We specialize in heavy-duty, rust-proof, and high wind-resistant rooftop structural engineering.

The Engineering Edge: We calculate exact tilt-angle parameters for maximum direct sunlight absorption and utilize strictly premium, weather-resistant DC/AC transmission cabling to eliminate transmission voltage loss.

D. Smart App-Based Solar Monitoring

Keep track of your technical energy generation metrics in real-time.

The Smart Edge: We integrate your intelligent solar inverter directly with your smartphone applications, allowing you to track exactly how many units (kWh) your roof is producing, analyze daily structural savings, and monitor system diagnostics from anywhere.

Our Solar Deployment Framework

1

Rooftop Audit & Load Assessment

We analyze your terrace diagnostics, shadow-free spacing thresholds, structural load capacities, and crosscheck your past 12 months of consumption bills to calculate the exact system size (e.g., 3kW, 5kW, or 10kW).

2

Bespoke Structural Fabrication

We construct highly resilient, elevated mechanical framing on your terrace, ensuring zero structural anchor leaks while keeping the underlying floor space completely usable.

3

Fixture & Inverter Commissioning

Deployment of Tier-1 Mono-PERC solar modules and smart grid-tied inverters, followed by full distribution box wiring equipped with secure surge protection devices and proper earthing spikes.

4

Net-Meter Integration & Approvals

We fully manage the official laboratory testing and deployment of the bi-directional net-meter by the state electricity board to securely initiate your grid savings log.

Frequently Asked Questions

Roughly 100 sq. ft. of shadow-free rooftop area is required per kilowatt (kW). So, a 3kW system needs around 300 sq. ft., and a 5kW system needs around 500 sq. ft.

Solar panels generate electricity based on light intensity, not heat. While production drops during heavy cloud cover or monsoons, they still generate around 20–30% of their normal capacity.

Premium solar panels come with a 25-year performance warranty, while smart inverters typically carry a 5 to 10-year warranty. It is a long-term asset with minimal maintenance (only regular cleaning with water is needed).
