Our Core Solar Services

A. On-Grid Solar Systems (With Net-Metering)

The perfect solution for residential homes to drastically reduce monthly electricity bills. Your system generation is tied directly to the government grid.

How it helps: Excess power generated during the day is sent back to the grid via a net-meter, giving you electricity credits that virtually bring your power bill down to zero.

B. Off-Grid & Hybrid Solar Systems

Ideal for areas facing frequent power cuts or for complete energy independence. Hybrid systems combine the best of both worlds — grid connection and battery backup.

How it helps: Stores excess solar energy in high-performance batteries, ensuring your smart home, lighting, and essential appliances run seamlessly during night hours or power outages.

C. Premium Structure Fabrication & Wiring

A solar panel is only as good as its foundation. We specialize in heavy-duty, rust-proof, and wind-resistant rooftop structural fabrication.

The Engineering Edge: We ensure correct tilt-angle alignment for maximum sunlight absorption and use strictly premium, weather-resistant DC/AC cabling to eliminate transmission power loss.

D. Smart Solar Monitoring

Keep track of your energy production in real-time.

The Smart Edge: We integrate your solar inverter with your smartphone app, allowing you to see exactly how many units (kWh) your roof is generating, your daily savings, and the system's overall health from anywhere.

Technical Setup: Our Process

1

Rooftop Assessment & Load Audit

We evaluate your roof space, shadow-free areas, structural capacity, and analyze your past 6–12 months of electricity bills to recommend the exact system size (e.g., 3kW, 5kW, or 10kW).

2

Custom Structural Fabrication

We build robust elevated structures on your terrace, ensuring zero roof leakage and keeping the space underneath usable.

3

Panel & Inverter Commissioning

Installation of Tier-1 high-efficiency Mono-PERC solar panels and smart inverters, followed by complete AC/DC distribution box wiring with proper earthing and surge protection.

4

Net-Meter Activation

We facilitate the official testing and installation of the net-meter by the electricity board to officially start your grid savings.

Frequently Asked Questions

Roughly 100 sq. ft. of shadow-free rooftop area is required per kilowatt (kW). So, a 3kW system needs around 300 sq. ft., and a 5kW system needs around 500 sq. ft.

Solar panels generate electricity based on light intensity, not heat. While production drops during heavy cloud cover or monsoons, they still generate around 20–30% of their normal capacity.

Premium solar panels come with a 25-year performance warranty, while smart inverters typically carry a 5 to 10-year warranty. It is a long-term asset with minimal maintenance (only regular cleaning with water is needed).
