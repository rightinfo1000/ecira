Our Specialized Illumination Systems

Ledure Nexa Magnetic Track Systems

Complete structural freedom to move, slide, or adjust light fixtures seamlessly without damaging the false ceiling grid. Available in three distinct sizes: 5mm, 10mm, and 26mm, in classic black and white finishes. Designed to fit every modern architectural space.

Intelligent Controls & Power Drivers

Take absolute control of your lights with seamless dimming interfaces and tunable CCT protocols (3000K to 6500K) via RF 2.4GHz remotes. Deployed with high-performance recessed isolated drivers (100W/200W) for optimal long-term circuit safety.

Ledure Divine COB Spotlights

Premium deeply concealed anti-glare spotlights featuring premium craftsmanship and true color rendering (CRI >90). Operates efficiently at 135 lm/w with robust surge protection up to 4KV. Housing available in Bronze, Steel, Black, and White.

Ledure Saga Premium COB Series

Engineered for elite aesthetic layouts and continuous grid ceilings. Provides flexible accent adjustments to create visually stunning layers of light over premium kitchen counters, bars, and dining areas.

Saga Versatile Illumination Layouts

Tailored to perfection with round, square, and rectangular architectural shapes supporting single and dual lens modules. Offers adjustable focus capabilities and high-fidelity CRI >90 color projection for commercial and high-end residential needs.

Ledure Olive Focused Beam COB

Elegance in every focused beam. Features an architectural aluminum housing with a sophisticated minimal trim. Deploys premium Bridgelux components to provide excellent glare-controlled illumination (CRI >80) for specialized focus pockets.

Ledure Boost High-Performance COB

Built to boost, designed to impress. Delivers powerful architectural luminance operating at 140 lm/w with advanced multi-groove heat dissipation grids. The ideal option for crisp illumination and grand room settings.

Ledure Casa Luxury COB Series

Bringing premium luxury and structural warmth into everyday living spaces. Available in an elegant Gun Metal finish, combining aesthetic appeal with heavy-duty durability to deliver true 7-star resort vibes inside your home.
