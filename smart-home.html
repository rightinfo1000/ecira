Our Core Expertise & Services

A. Architectural & Smart Light Fitting

Elevate your interiors with modern lighting layouts. We specialize in the flawless installation of profile LED lights, magnetic track lights, concealed downlights, and cove lighting.

The Smart Edge: Every light we fit can be integrated with smart controllers, allowing you to dim, schedule, or tune the colour temperature (warm to cool white) via your smartphone or voice assistants like Alexa and Google Home.

B. Smart Switchboards & Concealed Wiring

Safety meets sophistication. We handle complete, heavy-load-calculated concealed electrical wiring for new constructions and renovation projects, ensuring zero clutter and maximum safety.

The Smart Edge: We install elegant smart touch switches or retrofit hidden automation modules (smart relays) behind your existing switchboards, turning your standard fans and lights into smart appliances without changing your decor.

C. Customized Lighting Automation & Scenes

We program your electrical ecosystem to match your lifestyle. Enjoy customized lighting "scenes" tailored for different times of the day.

🎬

Movie Mode

One tap dims the living room profile lights, turns off the main ceiling lights, and prepares your space for entertainment.

🌙

Night Path Mode

Smart motion sensors gently illuminate walkways or bathrooms at 10% brightness during midnight hours, ensuring comfort and safety.

D. False Ceiling & Centralized Panel Wiring

Clean execution is our signature. We design organized, tangle-free wiring layouts inside false ceilings and set up centralized distribution panels that make troubleshooting or upgrading future smart devices completely effortless.
